Frequently asked questions

Is SettliA free?
Yes. The free plan includes up to 3 groups, unlimited members, splitting and balances, and 3 AI uses per month of each type (scan, voice and text). Pro adds unlimited groups, unlimited AI, recurring expenses and advanced stats.
Do my friends need the app?
Yes. Each member uses SettliA with their own free account, so balances stay in sync for everyone. You invite them with a link; it takes seconds to join.
How does the AI work? Is it reliable?
You type, speak, or photograph the receipt and the AI builds the expense. It always shows what it assumed and you confirm before saving — you have the final word.
Does SettliA handle the money?
No. SettliA works out who owes who and shows each person's payment details; you pay with your own banking app. We don't process or hold money.
Does it work for a flatshare?
Yes. Create the group as “Household”: continuous accounts, recurring expenses (rent, internet) and reminders to settle when it's time.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes: encrypted in transit, row-level security (you only see your own groups), and you can delete your account anytime. More in the privacy policy.
Can I install it on my phone?
Yes. SettliA is a PWA: open it in your browser and “Add to home screen”. App Store and Google Play apps are on the way.
